About Our Landscaping Heritage

Discover the story behind Aled Valley Landscapes – our roots, our vision, and our commitment to superior hard landscaping.

Our Story

Aled Valley Landscapes is rooted in a passion for transforming outdoor areas into functional and breathtaking spaces. Based near Gllansunen – just a short distance from Abergele and Denbigh – we extend our services throughout North Wales, including Chester, Holyhead, and beyond.

Our journey began with simple gardening projects, and over the years, we have evolved into a dedicated team specialising in hard landscaping. From driveways and patios to structural replacements and stable conversions, every project reflects our core values of quality, durability, and timeless design.

Our Approach

At Aled Valley Landscapes, we blend modern construction methods with a respect for traditional craftsmanship. Our process begins by understanding your specific goals – whether it’s creating a spacious driveway, a cosy patio, or a complete reimagining of your outdoor living space.

We meticulously plan each project, selecting premium materials that are suited to the British climate, from robust natural stone to long-lasting fencing. This attention to detail carries through to the construction phase, where our experienced crew adheres to best practices for safety, precision, and structural integrity.

Ultimately, our approach prioritises clear communication, excellent customer service, and results that endure.
